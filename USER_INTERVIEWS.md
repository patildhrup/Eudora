# User interviews

## Interview 1 — MZ

- **Role:** Head of Finance
- **Company stage:** Seed-stage SaaS startup
- **Quote:** "I know we are overpaying for Copilot and ChatGPT, but I don’t have a good way to quantify it."
- **Surprising insight:** They were more interested in a trusted audit link to share with the founder than in the numbers themselves.
- **Product changes based on interview:** Added a shareable report summary and a short explainer card for "why this number matters".

## Interview 2 — AK

- **Role:** Engineering manager
- **Company stage:** Pre-Series A AI startup
- **Quote:** "We spend most of our budget on API consumption, and I need a way to compare that to seat license costs."
- **Surprising insight:** They treated Copilot as a fixed operations cost, but were open to swapping parts of it for developer-owned prompts.
- **Product changes based on interview:** Added a separate API usage section and a fallback recommendation path for usage-based plans.

## Interview 3 — LS

- **Role:** Founder / COO
- **Company stage:** 15-person startup
- **Quote:** "I’d pay for this only if it felt like a finance-grade audit, not just another AI tool checklist."
- **Surprising insight:** They were skeptical of an AI-generated summary until they could see the underlying rule logic for each recommendation.
- **Product changes based on interview:** Added a transparency section in the audit report showing the core rule triggers and why each recommendation was made.
