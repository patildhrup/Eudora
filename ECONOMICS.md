# Economics

## Unit economics (MVP)

| Item | Estimate |
|------|----------|
| OpenRouter summary | ~$0.002–0.01 / audit |
| Resend email | ~$0.001 / lead |
| Supabase | Free tier → ~$25/mo at scale |
| Vercel | Hobby → Pro at traffic spike |

**Gross margin on free audit:** ~99% (compute-light).

## Credex conversion model

Assume:

- 1,000 audits/month post-PH
- 30% show Credex CTA (savings ≥ $500/mo)
- 5% of CTA viewers book call → 15 calls/mo
- 20% close → 3 Credex clients/mo
- $2,000 avg first-year contract value → **$6k/mo** from funnel (illustrative)

## Sensitivity

- If savings engine is too conservative → fewer CTAs → tune overlap rules, not inflation
- If too aggressive → brand risk → keep confidence labels

## Payback

Build cost ~1–2 eng-weeks. Break-even on Credex if **1–2** clients close from first PH wave.
