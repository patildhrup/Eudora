## FAQs

1. **How does Eudora calculate savings?**
   It uses deterministic rules for seats, plans, billing cadence, and vendor pricing to identify overspend, not vague AI predictions.

2. **Do I need to connect my accounts?**
   No. You can enter subscriptions manually and still get a real audit report.

3. **What vendors does it cover?**
   ChatGPT, Claude, Cursor, GitHub Copilot, Gemini, OpenAI API, Anthropic API, and common AI subscription bundles.

4. **Is the summary actually generated by AI?**
   Yes, but only after the audit numbers are computed. The summary is a narrative wrapper, not the source of the savings logic.

5. **Can I share the audit with my team?**
   Yes. Every audit generates a shareable URL that can be viewed without exposing your raw plan details.
